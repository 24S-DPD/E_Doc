About Us

Empowering healthcare providers with innovative EHR solutions

What is E Doc

E-Doc is a specialized platform designed for the medical community to manage patient records efficiently and facilitate online patient communication through video and chat.

Our Mission

To revolutionize healthcare by offering a specialized platform for the medical community, enabling efficient patient record management and seamless online communication through video and chat. Our goal is to empower healthcare providers and enhance patient care with innovative, high-quality, and user-friendly solutions.

Our Values

  • Efficient Patient Record Management
  • Seamless Online Communication
  • Innovation for clinic doctors
  • User-Friendly Interface
  • Simplicity and Ease of Use
  • Robust Security Measures
